Evil as Humans

They will lie to their parents, to their friends, to their children, to their people, to their leaders, and worst of all, they will lie to themselves. That's why it's so goddamn believable. They're always being watched so it's important to play the part at all times. That's why when they believe they have some privacy, where no one (important) notices who they are. They exhale and finally let loose all the noises and intentions they wish to spread to others but can't just yet. Not until they have achieved or very close to achieving their goals. The false confidence comes from their self-identification from deep within, that's who they are truly are, not whatever they use to be before. Words can and will fool, everyone. Because when meeting someone new, that's all we have to go on. They ultimate deciding factor of someone's evil intent, comes down to their actions and their following reaction.
