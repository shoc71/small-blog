Schizophrenic Power System

For a system like this to exist and function, there can not be elements of fantasy that should be introduced.

This exists in a real-world and follows the laws of reality as one-to-one without ever breaking any fundamental rules of physics.

The key difference is the characters that exist in this world intentionally push the boundaries of what is real and what is possible

The best example of this is Baki. A show about a son who's trying to defeat the world's strongest man, which happns to be his father. His father isn't any ordinary strong fighter either, he intentionally goes out of his ways to bully other fighters that even think they have a remote chance of fighting him as he sees fit, continuously fight many people at their strongest points just to prove how obselete their power is to him. Lastly, puts himself in very precarious situations that most men would never dare of trying even amongst the elite (i.e. Continuously fighting alpha males in the animal kingdom)

Baki on the other hand, needs to congure up certain levels of disbelief or hell even delusions and even mental blocks like lifting 1000 kgs when reality he's benching 225 lbs. He has imaginary fights with insects, dinosaurs, and even past fighters to push the limits of what is theortically possible.
